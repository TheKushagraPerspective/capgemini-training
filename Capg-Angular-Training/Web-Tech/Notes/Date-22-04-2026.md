form handling
1. template driven form

* logic is written inside the template
* FormModule
* import @angular/core
* small forms
* small logic
* scalable , testable
* custom validation is very difficult


2. reactive form (model driven form)

* most of the logic is written in ts component
* ReactiveFormModule
* import @angular/core
* large form
* complex form
* more testable and scalable
* custom validation is very easy
